# Concept Guide: The Rolls-Royce "Spectre" Experience

## Visual Language
- **Color Palette**: 
  - `Midnight Black (#0A0A0A)`
  - `Starlight Silver (#E2E2E2)`
  - `Deep Royale Violet (#1A0F2E)` (Accent)
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: *Playfair Display* or a bespoke Serif (Classic & Luxury).
  - Body: *Inter* or *Outfit* (Modern & Readable).

## User Journey
1. **The Arrival (Hero)**: A dark screen with a single "Spirit of Ecstasy" outline. On scroll, the car emerges from the shadows.
2. **The Craft (Features)**: Close-ups of the leather, the dashboard, and the engine. Each scroll reveals a new detail with a cinematic fade-in.
3. **The Power (Performance)**: A transition to a wider shot of the car in motion (simulated via scroll-sequence).
4. **The Invitation (Call to Action)**: A majestic final shot with a "Book a Private Viewing" button that features a high-end ripple and magnetic effect.

## Interaction Philosophies
- **Silence & Speed**: Transitions should be fast but feel heavy and purposeful.
- **Micro-Delight**: Every button hover should feel like pressing a physical luxury switch.
- **Hierarchy**: Imagery first, minimal text.
